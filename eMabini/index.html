What is eMabini?

Through the PUP Open University System eMabini Learning Portal, the multi-generational learners can enjoy the personal and spontaneous learning as they complete the courses without sacrificing their time for work and family. The learners have several choices on ways to comprehend the lessons that are presented in the virtual learning environment and it provides an affordable and portable education for all.
